From a birthday weekend in Tahoe to right now — a year of firsts, and one very good idea.

Ch. 01

How it started

June 5, 2025 · Tahoe

The weekend we met

Tahoe was about anything but what I expected. I came for a birthday weekend and left with something much more exciting. From wearing pants to bed to a casino smooch, it was an interesting first meeting, and by the end of it, unfortunately, I was hooked.

the first visit

Our first strip

When you finally did come up to visit, and you admitted it was actually to see me, that's when things really kicked off. We started one of my favorite traditions: our photo booth. The first one ever was at Jackson with Alex, and that was a hell of a start to the hilarious tradition now taking up over half my desk space.

August 2, 2025

Official.

We stopped pretending it was casual. Year one starts here.

Ch. 02

A year of firsts

first gym day

Mandatory posing

This was one of our very first gym photos together, in a hilarious series of events where obviously we had to include Hank. That first day brought you into the Bay Club and forced you through everything. It always makes me giggle, and while every gym photo tells a different story, this is one of my favorites from a nostalgia perspective.

Sweet Maple

The first of many breakfasts

Our first Sweet Maple together. Man, that was an interesting morning because it was the first time we cried when you had to leave. Sweet Maple became one of our favorite spots, because who wouldn't love fried rice ramen? But aside from that, it will always have a special place in my heart as our first brunch, and the place where we realized, oh shit, you fly home today. It is one of my most bittersweet memories from the beginning.

Salt & Straw

First scoops

Our first time at Salt & Straw together. Obviously, living on top of Salt & Straw meant we were going to make numerous trips, but it always makes me smile that we can connect over something as small and sweet as dessert. Trying the new flavors each month is something I look forward to, even if I may get yelled at for trying them before you sometimes. And the amount of ice cream that got all over our faces afterwards was hilarious.

the Ferry Building

Sample everything

Had to throw in our first Ferry Building trip together, since it has become one of our favorite spots. Who doesn't love the Ferry Building? It is beautiful, and man, we always over-purchase, over-eat, and have a good time. Whether it is soft serve, pastries, empanadas, the farmers market, or whatever else the Ferry Building might hold, we always end up with quality laughs and a little time by the water, especially when it is sunny outside.

sunset · Alta Plaza

The obligatory Alta Plaza walk

I feel like I had to include the obligatory Alta Plaza walk and photos. The amount of sunsets carrying you up the hill when you don't want to go up there, the little sweet treats we've gotten, the sandwiches we've eaten up there. And let's not forget, that's where you surprised me for the first time. So definitely a memorable spot for us in more ways than one. Love you.

Golden Gate Park

A picnic by the flowers

Our first picnic date is absolutely one that I will always remember. The sushi, sweet treats, and Dubai chocolate in front of the Conservatory of Flowers on the lush green lawn. It was absolutely stunning. There is something about Golden Gate Park on a good day that feels magical when I get to walk through it with you. We sat down, ate, enjoyed each other's company, and took in everything around us.

first trip · Disney

Disney (and near-death by roller coaster)

Man, I had so much fun at Disney, but it came with a lot of ups and downs, pun intentional. You tried to kill me on the roller coaster, but we also had tasty food and made some great memories. I don't think I'll ever quite get over your mother consistently laughing at me after seeing the photo of me passing out on the Incredicoaster. So that is always a memory I'll have, for better or for worse. Disney will always have a new special place in my heart because of your love for it.

Huntington Beach

Our first little getaway

Had to include Huntington Beach because I know how much you love it, and I think this was technically our first getaway together. We got our own room, and I know that hotel is really important to you. It was kind of the first time we had our own space together, we got some tasty food, and I accidentally ate your pot bar. Little did we know we were mere feet away from your favorite plushie Charizard, stranded there for months until we found him later. That was a blast, and let's not forget the amazing dinner looking out over the beach. That one was absolutely scrumptious.

Japantown

The first pack

Who would have known one tiny vending machine would have started an army? Literally. So I had to include what I think is the first photo of you getting a pack from Japantown. And boy, has the addiction ramped up ever since. Plushies, packs, you name it. We are fully underway on that, and it's been so much fun. Now I treasure Japantown, and going there with you has become one of my absolute favorite memories.

the families

Mama & Papa

Had to include the first time you met Mama and Papa at the same time, which I believe was also near Valentine's Day. We went to dinner and got the caviar chips that I swear you've told everyone about. But we also went to the Ferry Building because you love it so much, and we brought them there to sample some food. That is something I will always treasure, and also a lovely first where I hope there are many more. Even though Papa does not like to leave the house.

first Valentine's · Cotogna

The truffle incident

There is no way I'm going to include a timeline of us without including our first Valentine's Day together. I absolutely loved what we did for this. I know we had to technically celebrate it on an odd day at an odd time, but we made it work, and I thought dinner was so, so tasty. Yes, a bit of a slip up on the truffle, but honestly that made me enjoy the dinner about ten times more, not only because it was very tasty, but because we got such a good giggle out of it. We ended up paying for the truffle, so everything was good there, but man, I would love to go back to that restaurant and do it again. Maybe this time we intentionally order the truffle.

Hawaii · Michael's birthday

Paradise (mostly)

Another bittersweet memory I had to put in here is the Hawaii trip, because you were down bad and I am not even going to mention the incident. But boy, will I never forget that, and I don't think you'll ever live it down. The weather was fantastic. We got to go to The One for the first time and eat some super tasty food, go on a hike that we were positive about, and really take in the beauty and nature of Hawaii. I know that is something we'll be doing plenty more of in the future.

Samantha's birthday

Susie Cakes, all the flavors

I'm going to throw your birthday in here on the timeline too, because technically it was our first official birthday, and that is a big milestone. I was on prep and it pained me, but I ordered you every Susie Cakes flavor under the sun. We got to sit there and sample them, then go to dinner with your friends and extended family, and it was such a nice time. I really loved getting to celebrate you, go to Big Bear, have all of your friends there, and make it a multi-part trip for all the awesome things that you do and are.

prep season

Saint behavior

I had to put a special shout out here in milestones and firsts for our first prep together, and the first time you've seen my patience really wear thin on a consistent basis. I have to put this in here to say thank you for dealing with me and being there for me when things are hard, unpredictable, and volatile. I will always appreciate that because it is exactly what I need deep into prep. An honorable mention here as well.

first card show

Two Charmanders & a Wailord

For my own personal love, I have to throw this in as a milestone: our first card show together. I know this one was recent, but it was genuinely one of my favorite dates. I know you had some tough cramps, but other than that, I thought it was absolutely amazing. We got to walk around, see so many cool cards, get you some cards, get me some cards, negotiate, trade, and see all these plushies. I thought it was so cool to bring my childhood with you and get to fully immerse in it. Absolutely one of my favorite memories, so I have to throw it in here selfishly. And we came away with TWO Charmanders and a Wailord.

lately

Movie nights and Ghibli

Now that we have our big Mr. Totoro from the claw game and a number of Studio Ghibli movies under our belt, this one is for movie night in general, whether it is Disney, Studio Ghibli, or anything else. I have really come to appreciate and fall in love with our quiet movie nights, where we get to share things with each other, experience movies, and build core memories around something simple, fun, and relaxing while enjoying each other's presence. I had to throw this one in here as well, and I'm sure there will be many more plushies along the way.

Ch. 03

For you

August 2, 2026

To a tiny baby,

The tables have flipped. A year ago, I was struggling to convince you that being my girlfriend was a good idea. And now, look at this. The tables really have flipped. I'm the one who gets bugged daily. Where's my ring? When are we getting engaged? And I wouldn't trade that for anything.

It makes me laugh every time I see how far we've come. Nothing could have prepared me for this last year and what an awesome journey it's been to watch us grow and change for each other, and to really see all of the magnificent things that have come from our relationship.

Tahoe kick-started it all, and I'm glad we're getting that same experience again as a nice marker on our one year. There have been so many memories that I'm not even going to attempt to list out my favorite ones. Nearly everything we do together has become a memory for me.

I look forward so much to what we have in year two, continuing to grow and continuing to learn. And that is what's so amazing to me about us: the ability to grow, change, and love each other.

So here's to more Giants games, more Pokemon cards, more walks that I drag you on, more beaches, more mountains, more plane rides, more of everything. I love you with my whole heart.

— Big Baby

Here's to year two,
Tiny Baby.
