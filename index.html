vol. i · our first year

Michael & Samantha

One Year of Us

From a birthday weekend in Tahoe to right now — a year of firsts, and one very good idea.

scroll

Ch. 01

How it started

June 5, 2025 · Tahoe

The weekend we met

A birthday weekend in Tahoe. We did karaoke — which genuinely terrifies us both — and somewhere in a casino (neither of us gamble) we had our first kiss. Best bad idea ever.

the first visit

Our first strip

The first time Samantha came up to visit me, we squeezed into a photobooth. We've kept the tradition alive ever since — there's a strip for every era of us.

August 2, 2025

Official.

We stopped pretending it was casual. Year one starts here.

Ch. 02

A year of firsts

first gym day

Mandatory posing

Our first workout together — obligatory flexing included, with Hank loyally third-wheeling. Back when Samantha still came to the gym with me. (We remember.)

Sweet Maple

The first of many breakfasts

Our first breakfast there — and the start of a long, delicious habit. The ramen-fried-rice dish is officially Ours now.

Salt & Straw

First scoops

The first cones, instantly a tradition. Whatever else is going on, we will always make room for ice cream.

the Ferry Building

Sample everything

Where Samantha insists on buying one of everything so we can sit outside and taste our way through it all. Honestly? Flawless system.

sunset · Alta Plaza

I carried you up the hill

Our first sunset here — earned only because I carried her up the hill (she flat-out refused to walk). Alta's been a love–hate thing ever since. Mostly the hate is the hill.

Golden Gate Park

A picnic by the flowers

Our first picnic date, right by the Conservatory of Flowers. Takeaway sushi, far too many snacks, perfect weather. A flawless afternoon.

first trip · Disney

Disney (and near-death by roller coaster)

So much fun — minus the part where Samantha nearly ended me on the roller coasters. Great food, great chaos. (Ask us about Universal, Harry Potter world, and a certain accidental bathroom recording another time.)

Huntington Beach

Our first little getaway

Our first mini-vacation together. Great hotel, great weather, unbeatable vibes. We're built for this.

Japantown

The first pack

It started innocently — a vending machine in Japantown. A thinly veiled cover story for what we now know was the beginning of a full-blown Pokémon era.

the families

Momma & Poppa

First time meeting the parents — so naturally we marched everyone to the Ferry Building to sample our way through it. Obviously.

first Valentine's · Cotogna

The truffle incident

The best pasta of our lives at Cotogna. Samantha accidentally ordered the truffle — a gloriously expensive mistake, and worth every single cent.

Hawaii · Michael's birthday

Paradise (mostly)

A birthday trip to Hawaii. Beautiful, sunny, an absolute blast — and we will be saying nothing about the darker, grosser, 3am chapter of this trip. The good parts were perfect.

Samantha's birthday

Susie Cakes, all the flavors

The best person to celebrate. A surprise Susie Cakes tasting with an absurd lineup of flavors. Zero regrets.

prep season

Saint behavior

Samantha putting up with Michael mid-prep. For the record: handling an irritable, short-fused big baby was so much harder than this photo lets on. Certified patience.

first card show

Two Charmanders & a Wailord

We came home with two new Charmanders and one Wailord. One of Michael's all-time favorite dates, no contest.

lately

Room for Ghibli too

Disney made room for Studio Ghibli, and it's been growing on her week by week. Turns out the heart has space for both.

Ch. 03

For you

August 2, 2026

Samantha,

One year ago, we were still pretending we were being reasonable. Then Tahoe happened, then the photobooth, then breakfasts, Disney, Hawaii, cards, Ghibli, and all the tiny ordinary days that somehow became my favorite part.

You make my life softer, funnier, and more like home. Thank you for choosing me on the easy days, the ridiculous days, and even the prep-season days where I fully did not deserve it.

I love you. I am so lucky this is ours.

— Michael

Here's to year two,
Tiny Baby.
